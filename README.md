
### Components

#### 1. Data Encryption Module
- Encrypts sensitive case files at the source  
- Ensures compliance with privacy regulations  
- Prevents unauthorized access to raw legal data  

#### 2. FHE Analysis Engine
- Performs computations on encrypted plea bargaining data  
- Generates statistical metrics and fairness reports  
- Allows complex analysis while maintaining full confidentiality  

#### 3. Research Dashboard
- Secure, aggregated view of results for legal researchers  
- Supports trend visualization and policy evaluation  
- Provides insights without exposing sensitive case details  

---

## Why FHE Matters

- Enables computation on sensitive legal data without decryption  
- Protects defendant privacy and case confidentiality  
- Allows policymakers and researchers to study fairness objectively  
- Supports legal compliance and ethical handling of case information  

---

## Use Cases

1. **Judicial Fairness Assessment**  
   - Evaluate distribution of plea outcomes across demographic groups  
   - Identify potential biases affecting legal equity  

2. **Policy Research & Reform**  
   - Study the impact of existing plea bargaining policies  
   - Inform legislative or judicial reforms based on aggregated data  

3. **Confidential Comparative Analysis**  
   - Compare trends across jurisdictions or time periods  
   - Maintain confidentiality of all involved parties  

4. **Legal Analytics for Academic Studies**  
   - Facilitate research on systemic justice issues  
   - Generate anonymized metrics for scholarly publications  

---

## Security Features

- **Client-Side Encryption:** Case data encrypted before sharing  
- **Immutable Computation:** FHE ensures computations do not reveal raw information  
- **Aggregated Results Only:** Researchers see only statistical summaries  
- **Privacy by Design:** No personally identifiable information is exposed
